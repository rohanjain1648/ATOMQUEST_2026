How to Export as PDF

  1. Press Ctrl+P (Windows) or ⌘P (Mac)
  2. Destination → Save as PDF
  3. Layout → Landscape
  4. Paper size → A4
  5. Margins → None
  6. Scale → Default
  7. ✅ Check Background graphics
  8. Click Save
⚠ Make sure Background graphics is enabled — without it the dark backgrounds won't print.

Best results in Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge.

Scoring Engine

BRD Score Computation

4 intelligent formulas. Real-time. Zero manual calculation.

📈

Min — Higher is Better

Revenue · Growth KPIs

Score = (Actual ÷ Target) × 100

Target: ₹42L · Actual: ₹47L → Score: 111.9

📉

Max — Lower is Better

Quality · Defect Rate KPIs

Score = (Target ÷ Actual) × 100

Target: 5 defects · Actual: 3 → Score: 166.7

📅

Timeline — On-time Delivery

Project Milestones

Score = 100 − ((A − T) ÷ T) × 100

Due: Day 30 · Delivered: Day 33 → Score: 90

🛡️

Zero — Compliance

Safety · Zero-tolerance KPIs

Score = Actual === 0 ? 100 : 0

Safety Incidents: 0 → Score: 100 (Perfect)

Overall Score = Σ (Goal Score × Weightage) ÷ 100
